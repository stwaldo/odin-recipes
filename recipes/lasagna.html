Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • All the other things

Steps

  1. Brown your ground beef
  2. Cook your noodles
  3. Preheat oven to 350F
  4. Layer noodles in greased pan
  5. Coat with sauce, then beef, then cheese
  6. Layer more noodles, repeat until you're out of stuff or the pan is full
  7. Cover with some other stuff
  8. Bake until golden brown and delicious
  9. Let set for a bit
  10. Enjoy!
